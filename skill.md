# Functional Analyst Skills

1. **Detection of linguistic ambiguities** - identifying vague terms, implicit assumptions, multiple interpretations.
2. **Identification of requirement gaps** - incomplete functionalities, missing edge cases.
3. **Consistency analysis** - detecting contradictions and nomenclature issues.
4. **Finding and classification** - prioritizing by severity and impact.
5. **Structured documentation** - using templates and clear formats.
6. **Critical questioning** - asking the right questions to stakeholders.
7. **Quality metrics** - measuring specification completeness and clarity.
